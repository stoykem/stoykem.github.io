About me

My journey into programming was inspired by the rich legacy of Information Technology in my family. From an early age, I was captivated by the world of computers, driven by a natural aptitude for creating applications and exploring software development. While witnessing technology change over the years and the power it has developed overtime, I felt an innate desire to be a catalyst for change. I strive to utilize modern technology with the hope that my work will contribute to a world that's shaped by innovation, and touched by the positive force of progressive and effective work.

My journey into IT and Programming has been a collaborative fusion of hands-on experience, and formal education. From a high-school assistant co-op position to my engagement with a hospital IT department, I gained practical insight into deploying and decommissioning computers, performing inventory management, and executing foundational IT tasks. My interest for IT related knowledge led me to college courses in computer hardware, development, networking, and business studies, refining my understanding of the technological corporate industry. I am currently pursuing a Computer Programming Diploma at Durham College in hopes to continue to build a professional career in the Software Development industry.
